Why Pathway?

For Students

  • Clarity: clear scope, skills, and timeline.
  • Support: regular mentorship and feedback.
  • Portfolio: ship work you can showcase.

For Companies

  • Speed: post scoped projects in minutes.
  • Talent: find pre-vetted student contributors.
  • Value: prototype ideas with low overhead.

How it works

1

Create an account

Choose Student or Company and set up your profile.

2

Post or browse projects

Share a clear scope, skills, and timeline—or find a match.

3

Match and collaborate

Message, meet, do the work, and track progress.

4

Review and showcase

Get feedback and add outcomes to your resume.
